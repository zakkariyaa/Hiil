Books are life, books are power

Changing A Child's Life One Book At A Time

Hiil is a non-profit charity organization with the mission of bringing books and education to children in need.

See Our Works Veiw Us In Action

What do we do?

Founded in 2007, Hiil works in 43 countries to a various degrees. We mobilize political will and material resources to help countries, particularly developing countries, ensure a "first call for children" and to build their knowledge to form appropriate education system and deliver books and other materials to children and their families.
